WORK 01

TYPE: VIDEO GAME
CLIENT: CCP GAMES

EVE ONLINE UNIVERSE

Eve Online has been one of the greatest and most immersive space epics in gaming. I was tasked with expanding the universe to give the players a chance to design their own custom avatar for Eve Incarna, and to engage in fierce ground battles in Dust 514. My biggest contribution at CCP was to push the boundaries of the Eve universe while staying true to the original vision.

WORK 02

TYPE: VIDEO GAME
CLIENT: UBISOFT

UBISOFT

Ubisoft’s catalogue of games is truly staggering. I have had the privilege to work on Ubisoft marketing and box art for years. WORK 03

TYPE: VIDEO GAME
CLIENT: LIMBIC SOFTWARE

ZOMBIE GUNSHIP SURVIVAL

A sequel to the popular game, Zombie Gunship; the mandate was “console grade graphics on a mobile device.” My role was that of Art Director and Lead Artist, so I was responsible for shaping the visual style, setting the quality bar, developing the production pipeline, creating high quality game assets, and establishing and maintaining the outsourcing effort. I also designed the logo, various marketing assets, and worked on narrative and story.
RESPONSIBILITIES: ART DIRECTION, LEAD ART, ART CREATION, BRAND DEVELOPMENT, LOGO DESIGN, STORY CONTRIBUTION, OUTSOURCING MANAGEMENT.
